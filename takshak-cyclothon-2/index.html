Cyclothon

Takshak'18, The Technological fest of MACE, Kothamangalam has collaborated with Kochi Metro Railway Limited and culminated in a Cyclothon, on October 14th.

The Cyclothon is aimed at promoting recycling of resources and the usage of cycles and public transport amongst citizens.
