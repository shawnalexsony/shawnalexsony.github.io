About

MA College of Engineering

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering pioneered engineering education in central Kerala in 1961. Managed by Mar Athanasius College Association, Aided by the Government of Kerala, the college was first in Asia under Christian Management. It was also the first engineering college to get affiliated to the Mahatma Gandhi University. MACE, as it is popularly known, has grown by leaps and bounds since. It now has six full fledged departments


MACE Quiz Club

A dias to share and show, the MACE quiz club is what it does. The club metamorphoses the enthusiasts into quizzers of diligence. The activities of the club include, weekly meetings that kens the members into the art of quizzing. The club is represented in many quizzes that happen in the vicinity of Kerala.
Since it's formation, MACE quiz club has brought laurels to the college. The team representing the club, has grabbed the University quiz championship in the academic year 2017-18. You may also find our members stealing television shows that feature quizzing. Show thirst for knowledge and MACE quiz club will lighten you.
