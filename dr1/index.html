In a Glance

One day hands on workshop with individual mentors for each group. They will be pro pilots from DRK, with experience in both LOS and FPV flights. Workshop includes the basics of drones , building one from scratch , open discussion regarding the future and possibilities of drone technology . Discussion regarding fixed wings will be included if there are candidates with interest . Candidates will be given simulator training and those who are capable of grasping the controls will be allowed to fly a real drone with the help of a mentor. Those who are interested to fly will be allowed to fly in trainer mode . Candidates will be able to learn about basics to adavnce techniques of drones , live demonstration of aerial mapping , aerial videography , dropping mechanism etc will be included .
