IEEE DAY CELEBRATION 2017

A colorful, full day event was conducted as part of IEEE day celebrations on 7th of October, 2017. The events comprised of a formal Inaugral ceremony, sessions led by eminent personalities from IEEE kerala Section, Cakecutting, Release of IEEE MACE SB magazine "Technicae" and an informal game round. Mr. Aravind Kumar V and Mr. Jishnu Krishna were the guests of the day. Their interactive sessions could address on what we IEEEians needed to develop and know-in- depth about the opportunities that lay before us. They motivated the audience as they shared their experience as an IEEE member and how it has benefitted them in a lot many ways.
The afternoon session was planned for a Treasure Hunt game within the MACE Campus. All teams were set on fire as they ran about unlocking questions and performing tasks. The 4day event was made worth remembering for the entire team of IEEE MACE SB.

20th March 2017

AGM 2017

Annual general meeting of IEEE MACE SB ,conducted on MARCH 20th 2017 at SEMINAR HALL II was the first event under the leadership of the EXECOM 2017 .
AGM has a thunderous start with a talk by Mr. JOSEPH JOSHY. He is one of the beaming and amicable volunteer of the Kerala sub section.
Afterwards the Official meeting was started with a silent prayer to grasp the blessings from the god .CODE OF ETHICS was recited by MR ADHARSH (membership development chair, IEEE MACE SB) to motivate the members towards the real goals of IEEE.Then Mr. FELIX B KOOTTAKARA( vice chair, IEEE MACE SB) presented a short description about the IEEE MACE SB He gives an overview w about the current activities and achievements of our SB.
Prof. BABU THOMAS (Counselor, IEEE MACE SB) welcomed all to the function . Then he invited Respected Principal Dr. SOOSAN T GEORGE for the presidential address. 
Prof. ELIZABETH SEBASTIAN (advisor, MACE IEEE SB; HOD EEE department) delivered a special address.
Afterwards,The LOGO of IEEE MACE SB was revealed by the honourable principal. The logo which shows the pride and glory of MACE SB was created by Mr. ANAND UNNIKRISHNAN.
Our official website was launched by Prof. ELIZABETH madam. The website designed by Mr. JOSEPH ASHWIN is elegant and professional with its simplicity and beauty.
Then honourable principal and our advisor awarded certificates to the former EXECOM as a momentum for the recognition of their hard work. The ceremony is also an inspiration for the upcoming EXECOM.
Afterwards Mr. ALAN MATHEWS (former vice chair, IEEE MACE SB) delivered the felicitation speech.  Then MISS ANNA S LAL (former chair, WIE society, IEEE MACE SB) gives the felicitation address. 
Last but not the least Mr. BASIL JAMES (former, IEEE MACE SB) delivered the felicitation address. He guided the upcoming EXECOM and members to explore the vast possibilities of IEEE. He introduced the new EXECOM under the leadership of MISS SHILPA JOSEPH. She concluded the meeting by her vote of thanks .She expressed her thanks for those who keep up the SB and requested for everyone's kind support for her team.

26-28th August 2016

Kochi Hub Meet 2016

IEEE MACE had the privilege of hosting the Kochi Hub Meet(KHM) in the year 2016.Held from August 26th to 28th,this three day event saw the participation of nearly 240 participants from over 40 colleges. The Kochi Hub Meet 2016 one of the last events conducted, was applauded by the entirety of the engineering student community across the Kochi sub-section irrespective of trade and institute. The event which witnessed an overwhelming participation of students for workshops such as ruby on rails,3D blender,Open CV and Managerial handled by recognized companies and their research teams. In association with the event, we also had talk sessions rendered by eminent personalities such as Ladha Nair, technocrats, and entrepreneurs.Cultural night and an ice breaking session through a game session was also held. It's during these events that you really get to know and experience what IEEE is all about.How it's not some organization which conducts a truck load of workshops and talks but an arena which brings a set of common minded individuals together where you learn and grow together.
