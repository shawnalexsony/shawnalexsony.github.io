We are the NetX Club

We learn best in moments of enjoyment and the best way to learn is through sharing what we know. NetX is a venture that takes a step towards something just like that; a community of students passionate about development through learning.

With our motto "LEARN, DEVELOP, DEPLOY", we aspire to bring forth a knowledge sharing mechanism and spark informed conversation through peer to peer learning. NetX is a platform that hugely advocates FOSS, and hopes to have more of the student populace here, at MACE, become advocates for the same.
